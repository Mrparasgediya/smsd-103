Student Management System

  • Student Management System is a fully computerized system or a database where all the student related data can be stored, retrieved, monitored & analyzed.
  • The data is saved at a centralized location & role-based login access is given to all the stakeholders for ensuring student data security.
  • Functionalities like registration, admission, billing, financial aid provision to students make the entire process of enrollment quick, systematic & error-free
